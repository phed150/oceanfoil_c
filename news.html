Oceanfoil carries out model tests of its latest wing-foil design in the last week of May 2013.

Oceanfoil is now anticipating carrying out model tests of its latest wing-foil design in the last week of May, 2013. The Company has commissioned the building of two 1/50 scale models of their wing-sail rigs which will be installed on a 1/50 scale model of a 150,000 ton merchant vessel for trials which will take place on the Timsbury Lake testing facility of Southampton Solent University. This facility is used by Trinity House for the training of deep sea pilots and captains of vessels. The testing programme will last for four to five days and will allow Oceanfoil to fine tune design features of its latest wing-sail.
